template<typename T >
T util::savgol_cubic ( Sample_set< T > &  points)

Compute a Savitzky-Golay smoothed value of the set of data points provided.

The points represent a window and the SV is a form of average that makes a least-squares fit to the data. In this case we use a cubic polynomial fit.

Parameters
pointsSet of points to be smoothed.
Returns
Estimated value at the center of the window. {util-3.1}
